About Our Ministry

Life and Hope in Christ

New Life and Hope in Christ Jesus Ministry, aka Life and Hope, cleaves to a vision to spread the Gospel and help people experience the transformative power of Jesus Christ.

We are a vibrant, multicultural community of believers committed to making disciples, serving our community, and sharing God's love with the world.

Our mission is to lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ through worship, fellowship, discipleship, and service.
